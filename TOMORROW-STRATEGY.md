## Why 150 Codes Failed — Real Analysis

### It's NOT about leagues being "bad"
Georgian league had 17 losses — but also had wins. Ethiopian league cut WHFZ5X — but that was 1 loss in 14 games. Banning leagues entirely removes winning picks too.

### The real problems were:
1. **Too many games per slip** — 20-50 game accumulators are mathematically dead
2. **Exotic markets** — "Excluded Number of Goals", "2nd Half Team-Specific Over/Under", "Both Halves Over 1.5" — these failed hard
3. **Odds too high on some picks** — over 1.50 = 50% hit rate, over 2.00 = 0% hit rate in our sample
4. **No diversity in loss exposure** — same match appeared in 30+ of 50 slips, so one loss killed 30 slips

### Win rate by odds range (from our 430 settled games):
- Under 1.30: **84%** win rate
- 1.30-1.50: **80%** win rate  
- 1.50-2.00: **50%** win rate (coin flip)
- Over 2.00: **0%** win rate

## Strategy for June 23

### Core Rules

1. **Max 12 games per slip** — JDMNPB proved 10-12 is the sweet spot
2. **Max 1.45 odds per pick** — stay in the 80%+ win rate zone
3. **Target 50-500x total odds** — realistic, achievable
4. **Use diverse markets per match** — don't just pick Over 2.5 for every game:
   - Home or Over 2.5 (hits if home wins OR 3+ goals)
   - Double Chance & Over 1.5 (hits if either team wins AND 2+ goals)
   - 2nd Half Multigoals 1-3 (hits if 1-3 goals in 2nd half — very common)
   - Over/Under Early Goals (first 30 minutes)
   - Draw No Bet (refund on draw)
   - Home Team to Win Either Half (just needs to lead in either half)
5. **3+ punter consensus required** — if 3 out of 5 punters picked the same match, it's worth considering
6. **NO exotic markets**: Excluded Goals, Both Halves Over, Correct Score, team-specific 2nd Half markets
7. **Mix kickoff times** — some 1pm, some 3pm, some 5pm. Don't stack all games at the same time

### Generation Plan

**Phase 1: Build Pool**
- Fetch all punter codes
- Filter out started matches
- Filter out exotic markets (Excluded Goals, Both Halves Over, Correct Score)
- Filter out picks with odds > 1.45
- Build consensus map

**Phase 2: Score and Select**
- 3+ consensus = include
- 2 consensus + odds under 1.30 = include  
- 1 consensus from Tier 1 punter + odds under 1.25 = include
- Everything else = skip

**Phase 3: Market Optimization**
- Don't invent new markets — only convert within the same family:
  - Over 3.5 → Over 2.5 (same family, safer)
  - 1X2 Home → Double Chance Home/Draw (same family, safer)
  - BTTS Yes → Over 1.5 (similar concept, easier to hit)
- For each match, CHECK AVAILABLE MARKETS and pick the safest under 1.45 odds
- Consider alternative markets like:
  - "Home or Over 2.5" instead of plain Home Win
  - "Home Team to Win Either Half" instead of 1X2
  - "2nd Half Multigoals 1-3" instead of Over 2.5

**Phase 4: Generate Slips**
- **10 Banker slips**: 8-10 games each, all under 1.35 odds, 3+ consensus
- **10 Solid slips**: 10-12 games each, all under 1.45 odds, 2+ consensus
- **NO 20+ game slips** — they're mathematically dead
- Each match can appear in max 4 of 20 slips (20% cap)
- Diversify across different match combinations

**Phase 5: Flex Strategy**
- Every slip gets played with flex
- 8-game slip → 1 flex
- 10-game slip → 1-2 flex
- 12-game slip → 2 flex
- Stake: ₦10-50 per slip depending on confidence

### Target Outcomes
- 20 total codes generated (not 50, not 150)
- 10-12 games each (not 25-50)
- 50-500x odds per slip (not 1000x+)
- At least 2-3 clean wins or 1-flex wins expected
- Total stake: ₦200-500

### Key Insight
JDMNPB won with 25x odds and 11 games. That's the template. Small, tight, high-probability. Not moonshots.
