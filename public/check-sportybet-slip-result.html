How the Scanner Works

SlipPilot Scanner reads your SportyBet booking code and checks every single selection against the actual match result. In seconds, you see:

  • WON — the pick was correct
  • LOST — the pick failed
  • VOID — the match was postponed, cancelled, or the pick was refunded
  • PENDING — the match has not finished yet

You also get a hit rate percentage showing how many settled picks were correct. This is the single most important number for evaluating any tipster.
