FAQ

Is SlipPilot free?

Yes, completely free forever. No account needed, no hidden charges.

Which bookmakers do you support?

Currently SportyBet Nigeria. We update SlipPilot daily and are actively adding Bet9ja, 1xBet, and BetWay. Check back or submit a feature request to be notified when your bookmaker is added.

Can I convert Over 3.5 to Over 2.5 automatically?

Yes. Use the Convert tab. Paste your code, click Make Safer, and it converts all risky goal markets down one level and generates a new booking code instantly.

Will it change my odds?

Removing games reduces combined odds. Converting a pick to a safer market usually lowers the odds for that game but increases your chance of winning.

What is the Scanner?

Paste an old code after games have finished. SlipPilot checks each pick against the actual result and shows what won, lost, and went void.

What other sports does SlipPilot support?

Football, basketball, tennis, ice hockey, handball, table tennis and more. All markets available on SportyBet work, including throw-ins, corners, fouls, cards, and player-specific markets.

How often is SlipPilot updated?

Every day. We ship improvements, new filters, and bookmaker support continuously. Submit a request via the support form to suggest a feature and we will email you when it is live.

Can I track multiple punters?

Yes. Enter a punter name when scanning any code. SlipPilot tracks hit rates across all their slips and ranks them.
