FAQ

Is SlipPilot free?

Yes, completely free. No account needed.

Does it work with any SportyBet code?

Yes. Paste any booking code — active or expired.

Will it change my odds?

Removing games reduces combined odds. Optimizing a pick may change individual odds depending on the safer market.

What is the Scanner?

Paste an old code after games have finished. SlipPilot checks each pick and shows what won, lost, and went void.

Can I track multiple punters?

Yes. Enter a punter name when scanning. SlipPilot tracks hit rates and ranks them on the leaderboard.
